- **Sound Design:** Add a "hiss" sound effect for the spray and a "whirring" sound for the spinning chain to add realism.

---

## 📸 Production Tips
1. **Lighting:** Use a single harsh light source from the side (rim lighting) to highlight the metallic texture of the chain.
2. **Camera:** Shoot in 4K 60fps or 120fps if available. Use a macro lens for the opening shot.
3. **The "Green" Factor:** TC 40 has a distinctive green color. Ensure your lighting makes this color pop against the dark chain.
4. **Fast Cuts:** Edit on the beat of the music. Each transition should feel snappy and deliberate.
